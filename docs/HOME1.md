Purpose of This Page

The Home page is the entry point to the site and is designed to make an immediate first impression in under 5 seconds. Its primary goal is clarity over cleverness: clearly establish who I am, what I do, and where the visitor should go next.

This page is intentionally minimal, with strong typography and generous whitespace. It is not meant to overwhelm the user with content, but to act as a calm, confident introduction.
